OneDraz – Episode 1: Genesis of the Void

900 years ago.
Before time could be measured by men, the Seven Cosmic Entities emerged at the heart of existence. They were not born, nor created — they manifested from the fabric of Creation itself, each embodying an eternal force: Light, Darkness, Time, Space, Chaos, Order, and the Unknown.

Drawn to one place beyond the reach of mortal stars, these entities stood as the Rulers of the Cosmos and the Multiverse. To understand their purpose, they chose not to exist in separation. They raised their infinite hands of energy, merged their essence, and became One.

The union was overwhelming. The vast oceans of the multiverse trembled, constellations fell silent, and even dimensions folded. The presence of their collective form filled all of existence with a divine brilliance — so radiant that it drowned even shadows.

As they focused their energy into a single point — a glowing sphere of transcendent power — the entire cosmos quaked. Then came the Silence. Stars vanished. Worlds became dust. What remained was emptiness — an endless void stretching into nothingness. In their fusion, the Seven had given birth not to life... but to an Ending.

And within that endless emptiness, something ancient stirred, waiting to be awakened again…
